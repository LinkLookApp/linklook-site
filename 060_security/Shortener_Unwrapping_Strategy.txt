Shortener Unwrapping Strategy
Generated: 2026-03-08

Purpose
Safely expand shortened URLs before the final risk decision.

Supported examples
- bit.ly
- tinyurl
- t.co
- custom branded shorteners

Rules
- unwrap in a controlled environment
- limit redirects
- preserve full hop sequence
- classify both the shortener and the final destination
- cache safe expansions for performance where policy allows

Risk notes
Shorteners are not inherently malicious, but they remove destination transparency and are heavily used in SMS phishing.
