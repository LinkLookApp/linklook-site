Domain Similarity Detection
Generated: 2026-03-08

Purpose
Detect domains that imitate trusted brands or high-value targets.

Typical attack patterns
- character substitution
- missing or added letters
- visually similar characters
- brand plus security-related words
- bank name plus regional modifier

Example patterns
- paypaI.com
- rnicrosoft-login.example
- secure-apple-id-check.example

Detection methods
- Levenshtein distance against protected brand domains
- keyboard-adjacency checks
- token similarity on domain labels
- Unicode normalization before comparison
- suspicious keyword combinations such as login, verify, support, secure, update

Proposed output
- matched_brand
- similarity_score
- suspicious_tokens
- confidence

Operational rule
Similarity alone is rarely enough to block.
Similarity + new domain + login form + redirecting behavior can justify a block.
