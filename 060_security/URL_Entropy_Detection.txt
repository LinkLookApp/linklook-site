URL Entropy Detection
Generated: 2026-03-08

Purpose
Flag URLs with unusually random or machine-generated characteristics often seen in phishing, malware staging, or tracking chains.

Signals
- very long path or query length
- high ratio of random-looking characters
- excessive encoding
- many tracking parameters
- base64-like or token-heavy segments
- large number of nested redirects

Use cases
- suspicious login paths
- malware download links
- one-time campaign links
- obfuscated affiliate or redirect abuse

Output
- entropy_score
- dominant suspicious segments
- explanatory flags for debugging and user-facing review
