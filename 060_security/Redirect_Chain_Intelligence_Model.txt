Redirect Chain Intelligence Model
Generated: 2026-03-08

Purpose
Model and score redirect chains because many phishing and malware campaigns hide behind multiple hops.

Example chain
SMS link -> shortener -> tracker -> disposable domain -> phishing page

Features to record
- number_of_hops
- hop_domains
- domain_age_per_hop
- status_codes
- protocol changes
- geo or hosting changes
- whether a login page appears only at the final hop

Risk indicators
- multiple first-seen domains in one chain
- hop into known suspicious hosting
- sudden transition from trusted-looking domain to unrelated infrastructure
- final destination contains password form or payment request

Output
- redirect_risk_score
- resolved_final_url
- explanation of the most suspicious hop(s)
