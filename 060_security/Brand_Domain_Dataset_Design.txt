Brand Domain Dataset Design
Generated: 2026-03-08

Purpose
Define the protected brand dataset used for brand impersonation and typosquatting detection.

Priority brands
- Apple
- Google
- Microsoft
- PayPal
- major Dutch banks
- major EU banks
- national government and tax portals
- major package and delivery providers
- major telecom providers

Record structure
{
  canonical_domain,
  brand_name,
  category,
  region,
  importance_tier,
  known_legitimate_tlds,
  known_login_paths,
  notes
}

Governance
- Version the dataset
- Record source and date added
- Allow manual review of additions
- Keep a suppression list for false alarms

Why it matters
Without a protected dataset, similarity algorithms are much weaker and harder to explain.
