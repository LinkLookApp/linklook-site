First 24 Hours Domain Risk Model
Generated: 2026-03-08

Purpose
Capture the elevated risk associated with domains that are extremely new, especially in the first 24 hours after registration or first observation.

Why this matters
Many phishing campaigns rely on newly created domains before blocklists catch up.

Key signals
- registration age under 24 hours
- certificate first seen very recently
- no historical reputation
- disposable or cheap hosting patterns
- login or payment page behavior
- brand similarity

Decision guidance
Newness alone is not enough to block.
Newness combined with impersonation or credential harvesting signals should sharply increase risk.

Output
- new_domain_score
- age_bucket
- supporting evidence
