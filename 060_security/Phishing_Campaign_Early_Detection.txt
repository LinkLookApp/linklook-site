LinkLook — Phishing Campaign Early Detection
Generated: 2026-03-08
Origin: ChatGPT

Objective
Spot suspicious infrastructure before it becomes a mass campaign.

Candidate early signals
- newly registered domains
- certificate transparency issuance bursts
- domain names resembling brands
- repeated registrar / hosting patterns
- unusual redirect infrastructure
- suspicious AASA claims or changes

Practical use
Early-detection signals should raise scrutiny, shorten cache TTLs, and increase
escalation priority. They should not auto-block on their own unless reinforced
by stronger evidence.
