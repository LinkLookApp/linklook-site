1. Why intercept links?
   Because pre-navigation evaluation is the safety function.
2. Is personal browsing monitored?
   No. LinkLook keeps a local safety-check log on-device (URL, verdict, reason

   browsing history. The user can clear or disable it. No check history data
   leaves the device.
3. Does the app break normal browsing?
   No. Safe links open in LinkLook’s built-in browser. Suspicious links are
   explained in plain language. The user always chooses whether to proceed.
4. Does LinkLook claim perfect safety?
   No. Public language is about warning and risk reduction, not guarantees.
