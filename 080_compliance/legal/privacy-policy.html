Privacy Statement

Last updated: March 20, 2026

The short version: LinkLook is designed to keep control with the user. Some checks stay on your device, while others may rely on cloud processing or selected external providers, depending on the feature you use and the protection level you choose.

1. Who we are

LinkLook is a browser for iOS that helps people spot suspicious links early and make safer decisions online.

LinkLook BV is the controller responsible for the processing of personal data described in this Privacy Statement.

2. What this statement covers

This Privacy Statement explains what personal data LinkLook may process, why we process it, how long we keep it, when external providers may be involved, and what choices and rights you have.

3. The privacy choices in LinkLook

LinkLook gives you control over how checks are performed. You can choose one of three protection levels during setup and change that choice later in Settings.

Protection level What this means
On-device only No link or webpage data is sent to LinkLook’s cloud for analysis in this mode.
Cloud link check The link you check may also be processed by LinkLook’s cloud services for additional analysis. The webpage itself is not fetched for cloud analysis in this mode.
Cloud link and page check Both the link and relevant webpage data may be processed by LinkLook’s cloud services for deeper analysis.

4. What data we may process

Depending on the feature you use and your selected protection level, LinkLook may process:

  • the link you check
  • related webpage data needed for analysis
  • technical and diagnostic information needed to operate, secure, and improve the service
  • contact details if you contact us directly

We aim to limit processing to what is needed for the requested check or service function.

5. Why we process data

We may process data to:

  • perform link and webpage checks requested by the user
  • identify suspicious or malicious patterns
  • provide warnings, context, and analysis results
  • secure and maintain the service
  • respond to support requests
  • comply with legal obligations

6. External providers

Some checks in LinkLook rely on selected external providers. The exact mix depends on the type of check and your chosen protection level.

Provider 1

Retention — Not publicly specified at service level.

Location — Worldwide, including outside the user’s country.

Technical handling — Not publicly specified at service level beyond the request mechanics.

Compliance & oversight — Privacy framework commitments and regulatory oversight are stated.

Provider 2

Retention — Limited operational retention, with defined cache and backup windows.

Location — Multiple regions; not presented as one fixed processing location.

Technical handling — Encryption in transit and at rest, with restricted access.

Compliance & oversight — GDPR-aligned terms, certified hosting environments, and compliance or audit claims are stated.

Provider 3

Retention — Normally deleted within 30 days for API use, with limited exceptions for safety, legal, or policy reasons.

Location — Traffic may be routed across selected regions; stored data is kept in the United States.

Technical handling — Encryption in transit and at rest, with limited staff access.

Compliance & oversight — Commercial-use privacy commitments and independent certifications are stated.

Provider-specific details such as retention periods, processing locations, technical methods, and compliance frameworks may change over time under the providers’ own terms.

7. How long LinkLook keeps data

LinkLook keeps personal data only for as long as needed for the purpose for which it was collected, unless a longer period is required for security, legal, or operational reasons.

Where possible, we aim to minimize retention.

8. Sensitive data and user choice

LinkLook is designed to limit unnecessary sharing of sensitive information.

Depending on the feature, app version, and your selected protection level, certain checks may happen on-device before cloud processing takes place.

Where LinkLook offers warnings, masking, or additional choices before sending data for cloud analysis, those steps are shown in the app before the relevant data leaves your device.

Not every feature or check includes the same level of sensitive-data detection, masking, or user control.

9. International processing

Depending on the type of check and the providers involved, data may be processed outside your country and, where relevant, outside the European Economic Area.

Where required, appropriate safeguards are used.

10. Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, and to request data portability where applicable.

You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Statement or about privacy in LinkLook, you can contact LinkLook BV at privacy@linklook.app.

12. Changes to this statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time.

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
