Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026 — Version 1.0

The short version: LinkLook checks links to keep you safe. By default, everything happens on your device. Cloud features are optional, off by default, and you control them.

1. Who we are

LinkLook is developed by LinkLook (“we”, “us”). LinkLook is a safety-focused browser for iOS that checks links before you open them.

2. What data we collect

DataWhenPurpose
URLs you checkOnly when you enable Cloud URL CheckAI analysis for scam detection
Page content (fetched server-side)Only when you enable Safe Preview or Deep Page AnalysisVisual preview / AI page inspection
Browsing historyOn-device onlyRecent checks list — never sent to our servers
Favorites & Reading ListOn-device onlyYour bookmarks — never sent to our servers
Crash reportsOnly if you opt in via iOS SettingsFixing bugs

3. Three protection levels — you choose

LinkLook offers three protection levels. You choose during setup and can change anytime in Settings.

LevelWhat leaves your deviceDefault
On-device onlyNothingYes (default)
Cloud link checkThe URL you checkOff
Cloud link & page checkThe URL + the page is fetched by our serverOff

Each cloud level requires explicit confirmation before activation. You can turn any level off at any time in Settings → Enhanced Cloud Protection.

4. How we use your data

  • Security analysis only. URLs and page content sent to our cloud are used exclusively to check for scams, phishing, and malicious content.
  • No advertising. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.
  • No tracking. LinkLook does not track you across apps or websites. NSPrivacyTracking is set to false.

5. Third parties

  • Google Safe Browsing (GSB). LinkLook queries the Google Safe Browsing API to check URLs against known threat lists. Google receives a partial URL hash (not the full URL). Google’s privacy policy applies to this data.
  • LinkLook cloud servers. When you enable cloud features, URLs and/or page content are processed on LinkLook’s own servers. We do not share this data with other parties.
  • No other third-party SDKs that collect user data are included in LinkLook.

6. Data retention

DataRetention
On-device historyUp to 100 entries, stored on your device only. You can clear it anytime.
Cloud URL analysisServer-side logs are retained for up to 90 days, then automatically deleted.
Page content (fetched)Not stored permanently. Processed in memory and discarded after analysis.
Audit logMinimal audit events (no raw URLs) retained for 90 days.

7. Your rights

  • Control. You can disable any cloud feature at any time in Settings.
  • Delete. Clear your on-device history in Settings → History. Clear cloud analysis history in Settings → Enhanced Cloud Protection.
  • Access. All your data is visible in the app (Recent tab, Settings).
  • Withdraw consent. Turn off any cloud toggle to immediately stop sending data.
  • Data portability / erasure requests. Contact us at privacy@linklook.app.

8. Sensitive data protection

Before any URL is sent to our cloud, LinkLook scans it on-device for sensitive information (email addresses, phone numbers, tokens, health data). If detected, you are warned and can choose to mask, skip, or proceed. This happens before any data leaves your device.

9. Children’s privacy

LinkLook does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences focused on online safety.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated through the app. The “effective date” at the top reflects the latest version.

11. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at privacy@linklook.app.
