Transparency and Trust Model
Generated: 2026-03-08

Purpose
Define how LinkLook can credibly demonstrate that it protects users without becoming a privacy risk.

Trust pillars
- minimal data collection
- documented architecture
- independent review or audit where feasible
- clear user-facing explanations
- strict retention policy
- separation between security metadata and personal content

Proof mechanisms to pursue
- publish data flow summaries
- document what is never collected
- maintain audit-ready logging of system decisions
- consider third-party security and privacy review
- open selected non-sensitive components or specifications where strategically useful

Important boundary
Openness should increase trust without exposing secrets that materially weaken defensive effectiveness.
