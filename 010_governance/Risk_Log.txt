LINKLOOK — RISK LOG
Version: 2026-03-10
Origin: ChatGPT, updated to align with Canonical Master Decision Log 2026-03-10

Purpose
Track operational product, market, reputation, and execution risks in one place
without duplicating full analysis text across documents.

================================================================================
RISK 001 — COLD START / NO USER MOMENTUM
================================================================================
Description:
LinkLook may be technically strong but fail to reach enough users to generate
learning, trust stories, and distribution momentum.
Likelihood: High
Impact: High
Mitigation:
- keep v1 free
- optimise App Store discovery
- publish problem-solution website pages
- focus on family / elderly protection wedge
- treat early user stories as core growth assets
Owner: Founder
Status: Open

================================================================================
RISK 002 — EXPECTATION MISMATCH / REPUTATIONAL DAMAGE
================================================================================
Description:
If public claims imply perfect protection, a miss may cause outsized trust
loss even if the architecture is within normal security-product limits.
Likelihood: Medium
Impact: High
Mitigation:
- use “reduces risk” language
- use “checks links before they open” language
- avoid “prevents all scams” language
- explain boundaries honestly in onboarding and marketing
Owner: Founder
Status: Open

================================================================================
RISK 003 — FALSE POSITIVES ON LEGITIMATE LINKS
================================================================================
Description:
Incorrect blocking or warning on legitimate destinations can create rapid
uninstalls and poor reviews.
Likelihood: Medium
Impact: High
Mitigation:
- warnings over hard blocks unless confidence is very high
- measure false positive rate from TestFlight onward
- maintain clear override / safe return behaviour where appropriate
Owner: Founder / Product
Status: Open

================================================================================
RISK 004 — PRODUCT LOOKS LIKE SPYWARE
================================================================================
Description:
Because LinkLook inspects links before navigation, some users may assume the
app reads private browsing or tracks traffic.
Likelihood: Medium
Impact: High
Mitigation:
- privacy-first App Store copy
- plain language in onboarding
- minimise stored data architecturally
- publish privacy architecture summaries
Owner: Founder / Compliance
Status: Open

================================================================================
RISK 005 — PRODUCT FEELS INACTIVE
================================================================================
Description:
Users may install LinkLook, experience no visible warnings, and conclude that
it does nothing.
Likelihood: High
Impact: Medium
Mitigation:
- activity summaries such as links checked this week
- visible but calm proof-of-work screens
- educational onboarding that explains safe-path behaviour
Owner: Product
Status: Open

================================================================================
RISK 006 — NETWORK EXTENSION DELAYS FIRST MARKET ENTRY
================================================================================
Description:
Pursuing >80% protection in the first release may materially delay launch due
to entitlement, engineering, privacy, and test complexity.
Likelihood: High
Impact: High
Mitigation:
- V1 is a straightforward, security-driven browser without Network Extension
- Network Extension is planned for V3, after Action extension (V2)
- this risk is mitigated by the canonical roadmap (MDL-081)
Owner: Founder / Architecture
Status: Mitigated by canonical roadmap

================================================================================
RISK 007 — DISCOVERY FAILURE AFTER NEAR-SCAM SEARCHES
================================================================================
Description:
A person searches for an iPhone scam-link solution but does not find LinkLook
in App Store or search results.
Likelihood: Medium
Impact: High
Mitigation:
- App Store ASO for plain-language searches
- simple search-optimised website pages
- editorial mentions on scam-prevention topics
Owner: Founder / Marketing
Status: Open

================================================================================
RISK 008 — AI COST / COMPLEXITY OUTRUNS VALUE
================================================================================
Description:
AI classification may become too expensive or too central to the product,
creating latency, cost, or reliability problems.
Likelihood: Medium
Impact: Medium
Mitigation:
- deterministic checks first
- AI only for ambiguous or higher-risk cases
- log structured data for future SLM training
Owner: Architecture
Status: Open


================================================================================
RISK 009 — NETHERLANDS-FIRST POSITIONING GETS LOST
================================================================================
Description:
The repo and messaging may drift toward generic global language and lose the
clarity of a focused Netherlands-first launch strategy.
Likelihood: Medium
Impact: Medium
Mitigation:
- keep Netherlands-first language in business and product strategy docs
- prepare Dutch PR and launch assets early
- treat Dutch validation as the first market milestone
Owner: Founder / Marketing
Status: Open

================================================================================
RISK 010 — APP REVIEW MISUNDERSTANDS THE PRODUCT ROLE
================================================================================
Description:
Apple may misread LinkLook as a browser replacement, proxy, or surveillance
product rather than a pre-navigation safety layer.
Likelihood: Medium
Impact: High
Mitigation:
- maintain dedicated App Review notes
- explain browser hand-off clearly
- state privacy constraints and non-VPN behaviour plainly
Owner: Founder / Compliance
Status: Open

================================================================================
RISK 011 — DEFAULT BROWSER ENTITLEMENT REFUSAL OR RESTRICTION
================================================================================
Description:
Apple may refuse, narrow, or reinterpret the default browser entitlement path
needed for LinkLook’s browser model.
Likelihood: Medium
Impact: High
Mitigation:
- maintain entitlement documentation and review defense
- preserve reusable decision-engine architecture
- browser entitlement approval risk is an accepted showstopper risk (MDL-079)
- the minimal-browser fallback direction has been superseded (MDL-007);
  entitlement refusal would require strategic reassessment
- avoid coupling core product value to one platform approval path
Owner: Founder / Product / Architecture
Status: Accepted showstopper risk (MDL-079)

================================================================================
RISK 012 — TEST DISCIPLINE ERODES DURING BUILD PRESSURE
================================================================================
Description:
As implementation accelerates, developers may bypass test-first discipline,
causing regressions and reduced confidence in security behavior.
Likelihood: Medium
Impact: High
Mitigation:
- explicit TDD policy
- CI merge gate
- regression-test requirement for every important bug
- fixture requirement for rule changes
Owner: Engineering
Status: Open
