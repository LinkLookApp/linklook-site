LINKLOOK — GROWTH STRATEGY
Version: 2026-03-08
Origin: ChatGPT

SUMMARY
LinkLook should grow by becoming easy to discover at the exact moment a person
becomes aware of scam-link risk.

CORE THESIS
The best early growth trigger is not generic advertising. It is the near-scam
moment: a user or family member feels relief, then searches, tells others, or
installs protection for a relative.

PRIMARY GROWTH LOOPS

1. Post-scare search loop
near scam -> search for solution -> find LinkLook -> install

2. Family protection loop
elderly relative almost scammed -> adult child searches -> installs LinkLook ->
recommends to siblings / friends

3. Near-miss sharing loop
LinkLook warns -> user feels relief -> user tells others -> installs increase

4. Visibility loop
LinkLook checks links quietly -> user sees activity summary -> trust grows ->
retention improves -> more chance of future sharing

KEY GROWTH DECISIONS
- Keep v1 free
- Optimise App Store and website copy for problem-solution search intent
- Make the product easy to explain in one sentence
- Use calm, credible warning UX
- Avoid overclaiming

FIRST-YEAR PRIORITY
Discovery and trust before monetisation.


MARKET FOCUS
Primary beachhead market: Netherlands.
Use the Dutch market for trust validation, first PR, first user stories, and
family-protection distribution loops. Keep the assets broadly reusable for
English-speaking expansion after validation.

PRESS MOMENTS
Approach journalists at three moments:
1. launch of the first public version
2. first real interception / near-miss proof story
3. during visible scam waves already in the news
