LinkLook UX Principles
Version: v1.0
Date: 2026-03-08
Origin: ChatGPT

1. Warnings must be rare and meaningful.
2. Safe links must feel fast and low-friction.
3. Messages must use human language, not technical jargon.
4. Dangerous links must default to the safer action.
5. The user must understand why the warning appears.
6. The experience should feel calm and trustworthy, not alarmist.
7. Accessibility must be considered from the start, especially for elderly users.
